[h1] [b]Configure the camera how you like it! [/b] [/h1]

This mod allows you to extend the zoom range of the camera. It also provides additional customization options those who want to really customize their camera:
	
[h1]Zoom[/h1]
[list]
[*][b]Min Camera Distance[/b] - How far the camera can zoom in.
[*][b]Max Camera Distance[/b] - How far the camera can zoom out.
[*][b]Zoom Step[/b] - How fast the camera zooms.
[/list]

[h1]Angle[/h1]	
[list]
[*][b]Min Distance Pitch[/b] - The angle of the camera when zoomed all the way in.
[*][b]Max Distance Pitch[/b] - The angle of the camera when zoomed all the way out.
[/list]

[h1]Extras[/h1]
[list]
[*][b]Clouds[/b] - Enable or disable the clouds that appear when zoomed all the way out.
[*][b]Knobbly Tree Leaves[/b] - Enable or disable the Knobbly tree leaves overlay
[*][b]FOV (EXPERIMENTAL)[/b] - The field of view of the camera
[/list]


This mod was inspired by coryinthedank's Zoom+ mod. If you want, you can check out his mod here:
https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=1781410139

I wrote this mod to provide configuration options for the camera as well as to reduce how far down the Knobbly Tree leaves are on the screen.
